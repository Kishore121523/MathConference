International Conference on Wavelet Analysis and Graph Theory

September 15th and 16th, 2022

- The objective of the Conference is to promote research and contribute to the development of Wavelets and Graph Theory.
- The Conference will be conducted in blended mode (Online and Offline) according to the convenience of the participants.
- The Conference provides:
a platform for researchers, students and Teachers of higher education to share their knowledge, experience and research findings.
opportunities for the researchers to exchange innovative ideas in wavelet theory and graph theory.
an opportunity to young researchers to acquire collaborators and establish research relationship across the globe.

Call For Papers

Original research papers in all branches of Mathematics are invited for presentation in the conference. Papers will be accepted for presentation and publication only after passing scrutiny and peer review process. Publication will be carried out as per rules and regulations of the Journals. We solicit high quality original research papers in all branches of mathematics with scope for applications. All accepted papers which are presented in the conference will be considered for publication in the Scopus indexed Journals(Charges apply).
Contributors are requested to forward their papers(in word/Tex.format) to the organizing Secretary by email: icwagt2022@sastra.ac.in on or before 25.8.2022
